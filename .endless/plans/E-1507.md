## Relation-type vocabulary by pair

The CLI dispatcher validates `relation_type` against the legal set for the detected pair.

| Pair                | Legal `relation_type` values                                                |
|---------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| decision â†’ task     | `documents`, `cleans_up_by`, `implemented_by`, `relates_to`                 |
| decision â†’ decision | `reverses`, `modifies`, `documents`, `relates_to` (+ future `clarifies`, `reaffirms`) |
| task â†’ decision     | `implements`, `cleans_up`, `documents`, `relates_to`                        |
| task â†’ task         | `blocks`, `implements`, `replaces`, `documents`, `cleans_up`, `relates_to` (unchanged) |

Illegal type for the detected pair surfaces a `ClickException` listing the legal set.
