# E-1182 — Auto-inject shell-init in `endless-sandbox enter`

This task is bundled with **E-1164** (route helpers through worktree CLI). The full plan, rationale, files modified, and verification steps for both tasks live in:

→ [`E-1164.md`](./E-1164.md) (Piece B: "Auto-inject in `cmd/endless-sandbox/enter.go`")

Why bundled: each task has limited user-visible value alone. Routing without auto-inject only helps users who manually `eval "$(endless shell-init)"` in their rc. Auto-inject without routing means the wrong `endless` (the global one on PATH) is called from inside a sandbox subshell, defeating the point of running there.

Single pointer instead of duplicating the plan: avoids two copies drifting. If you change the plan, change `E-1164.md` only.
